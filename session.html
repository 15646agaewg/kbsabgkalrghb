What is Session?

Session is an end-to-end encrypted messenger that protects your private data.
A decentralized app designed, built, and operated by a global community of privacy experts.

Privacy Benefits

No Phone Numbers

Keep your identity private with Account IDs. No phone or email required.

No Data Breaches

Session doesn’t collect your private data, so there’s nothing to leak.

Safe Paths

Onion-routed paths protect your conversations from eavesdroppers.

Open Source

Anyone can view, audit, and contribute to Session's code.

People Powered

Thousands of nodes run by a global community.

No Trackers

Your data is never collected, tracked, or sold.
